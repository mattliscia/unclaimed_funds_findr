Oregon Unclaimed Property

The Oregon State Treasury's Unclaimed Property Program works diligently to reunite Oregonians with their lost or forgotten assets. With millions in unclaimed property waiting to be claimed, the program serves as a central repository for abandoned financial assets from across the state.

Search Oregon Unclaimed Property

Types of Unclaimed Property

Common types of unclaimed property in Oregon include:

  • Dormant bank accounts
  • Uncashed payroll checks
  • Insurance proceeds
  • Utility deposits
  • Stock certificates and dividends

How to Search and Claim Your Property

Step 1: Search the Database

Visit the Oregon Unclaimed Property website to search by name or business name.

Step 2: File Your Claim

If you find property belonging to you, follow the online instructions to submit your claim through the secure portal.

Step 3: Verify Your Identity

Provide the required documentation to prove your identity and ownership of the property.

Important Information

There is no time limit for claiming your property in Oregon

The service is completely free - there is no charge to search for or claim your property
