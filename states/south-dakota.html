South Dakota Unclaimed Property

The South Dakota State Treasurer's Office, led by Josh Haeder, is dedicated to reuniting individuals, businesses, and organizations with their unclaimed property. Each year, millions of dollars in unclaimed assets are turned over to the state, and the Treasurer's Office works diligently to return these funds to their rightful owners.

Types of Unclaimed Property

Unclaimed property can include a variety of assets such as:

  • Forgotten bank accounts
  • Uncashed checks
  • Insurance benefits
  • Tax refunds

How It Works

These assets are reported to the state after a specified period of inactivity, and the Treasurer's Office becomes the custodian of these funds until they are claimed. The process is designed to be:

  • Secure and protected
  • Simple and straightforward
  • Completely free of charge

How to Search and Claim Your Property

Step 1: Search the Database

Visit the South Dakota Unclaimed Property database to search by name or business name.

Step 2: File Your Claim

Once you identify unclaimed property, filing a claim is simple and straightforward through the secure online portal.

Step 3: Provide Documentation

Submit the required documentation to verify your identity and ownership of the property.
