Maryland Unclaimed Property

The Comptroller of Maryland manages the Unclaimed Property Program, which allows individuals, businesses, and heirs to reclaim lost financial assets. Maryland now offers an online claims process, as well as a mail-in option for those who prefer traditional filing.

Search Maryland Unclaimed Property

How to Search for and Claim Your Property

Step 1: Search for Property

Use the Maryland Unclaimed Property Database to check if any unclaimed funds belong to you, your business, or a deceased relative.

Step 2: Submit a Claim

Complete the COT ST912 claim form (PDF) and follow specific instructions based on your claim type:

  • Individuals
  • Businesses/Organizations
  • Deceased Estates
  • On Behalf of Another Person
  • Joint Accounts/Multiple Owners

Step 3: Provide Documentation

Sign the form and attach all supporting documentation, such as proof of identity and ownership.

Step 4: Submit Your Claim

Submit via email or mail to the address listed on the claim form.
