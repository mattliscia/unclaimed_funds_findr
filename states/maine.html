What Is Unclaimed Property?

Unclaimed property includes:

  • Payroll & refund checks
  • Bank accounts
  • Stocks & dividends
  • Life insurance payouts
  • Safe deposit box contents

Each year, businesses turn over tens of millions of dollars in abandoned assets, which remain available free of charge until claimed.

How to Search for and Claim Your Property

Step 1: Search for Property

Use the Maine Unclaimed Property Database to search by name or business.

Step 2: Submit a Claim

If you find property belonging to you, follow the online instructions to submit your claim.

Step 3: Provide Documentation

Submit the required proof of identity and ownership documentation to verify your claim.

Step 4: Track Your Claim

Monitor the status of your claim through Maine's online claim tracking system.
