Mississippi Unclaimed Property

The Mississippi State Treasurer's Office manages the state's unclaimed property program. Each year, millions of dollars in unclaimed property are reported to the state, waiting to be claimed by rightful owners.

Search Mississippi Unclaimed Property

Types of Unclaimed Property

Common types of unclaimed property in Mississippi include:

  • Dormant bank accounts
  • Uncashed payroll checks
  • Insurance proceeds
  • Safe deposit box contents
  • Utility deposits
  • Stock certificates and dividends

How to Claim Your Property

Step 1: Search the Database

Visit the Mississippi State Treasury website to search for unclaimed property using your name or business name.

Step 2: File Your Claim

If you find property belonging to you, complete the online claim form or download and print the paper claim form.

Step 3: Submit Required Documents

  • Valid government-issued photo ID
  • Proof of Social Security number
  • Documentation showing your right to claim the property
  • Proof of address during the time the property was reported

Need Help?
