Washington D.C. Unclaimed Property

The Office of the Chief Financial Officer (OCFO) manages unclaimed property in Washington, D.C. In FY 2024, they successfully returned 39,351 properties totaling $35,094,859.86 to their rightful owners.

Drop Box Available: Monday–Friday, 8:00 AM – 4:30 PM in building lobby

Note: No walk-ins or same-day appointments are allowed

How to Search and Claim Your Property

Step 1: Search the Database

Visit the D.C. Unclaimed Property Web Portal and enter your last name and first initial.

Step 2: Verify Property

Ensure you are associated with the listed address. Do not add properties or addresses you are not associated with to your claim.

Step 3: Submit Your Claim

Submit your claim online through the Web Portal for faster processing and upload supporting documents through the secured portal.
