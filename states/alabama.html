What Is Unclaimed Property?

Unclaimed property includes assets that have been abandoned or forgotten by their rightful owners. In Alabama, this could include:

  • Unclaimed wages or paychecks
  • Dormant bank accounts
  • Utility deposits
  • Insurance proceeds
  • Stock dividends or refunds

How to Search for and Claim Your Funds

Step 1: Conduct a Search

Visit Alabama's official unclaimed property website

Enter your name (or business name) to see if you have any unclaimed assets.

Step 2: Review Your Properties

Once you've identified assets linked to your name, carefully review them. Select the properties you want to claim, then click File Claim to begin the process.

Step 3: Provide Your Information

  • Include your current mailing address (this is where your check will be sent)
  • Indicate your relationship to the property (e.g., "Owner," "Heir," or "Representative")
  • Verify the information before proceeding to the next step

Step 4: Confirm Your Address

If prompted, verify your address to ensure your check is mailed to the correct location.

What Happens After Filing a Claim?

Step 1: Review Documentation Requirements

Each claim requires supporting documents such as:

  • A completed and signed claim form
  • A copy of a government-issued photo ID
  • Proof of your Social Security number

Step 2: Submit Your Documents

Send all required documentation by mail to:

RSA Union Building
100 North Union Street, Suite 636
Montgomery, AL 36104

Step 3: Check Your Claim Status

You can track your claim at any time by entering your claim number on the Claim Status Page.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is there a fee to claim funds in Alabama?

No, the entire process is free. Beware of third-party services charging fees for this free process.

2. How long does it take to receive funds?

Processing times vary based on the complexity of the claim and the documentation provided.

3. Can non-residents claim funds in Alabama?

Yes! Even if you no longer live in Alabama, you can claim property as long as it belongs to you or your business.
