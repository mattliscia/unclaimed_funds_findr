Money Match Program

Washington's Money Match Program is reuniting people with their missing property without requiring them to take any action. If you're eligible, the state will automatically match your information with unclaimed property and send you the funds.

If you've received a postcard, you can simply enter the name or Property ID listed on the postcard to check if any of your funds are being returned.

Types of Unclaimed Property

  • Forgotten bank accounts
  • Uncashed checks
  • Insurance policy proceeds
  • Utility deposits
  • Stocks and bonds
  • Other unclaimed assets
