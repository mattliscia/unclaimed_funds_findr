Unclaimed Property in Idaho

The Idaho State Treasurer's Office manages the state's Unclaimed Property Program, connecting individuals and businesses with lost or forgotten financial assets. Idaho's unclaimed property program makes it easy to search and claim assets through its secure online system.

Search Idaho Unclaimed Property

What Is Unclaimed Property?

Unclaimed property includes financial assets such as:

  • Dormant bank accounts
  • Uncashed checks
  • Stock dividends
  • Refunds or overpayments
  • Insurance proceeds
  • Safe deposit box contents

These assets are transferred to the state when the owner cannot be located after a period of inactivity.

How to Search for and Claim Your Property

Step 1: Search for Property

Visit Idaho's unclaimed property database to search for your name or business name.

Step 2: Select Property and Begin Your Claim

Review the search results and select the property you wish to claim. Follow the on-screen instructions to start the claim process.

Step 3: Provide Supporting Documents

Required documentation typically includes:

  • A copy of your state-issued photo identification
  • Proof of your connection to the property (utility bill, bank statement, etc.)

Step 4: Submit and Monitor Your Claim

Use the online system to upload your claim form and documents, or mail them to the address provided on the claim form. You can monitor the status of your claim through Idaho's online claim portal.
