Missouri Unclaimed Property

Missouri's State Treasurer holds over $1 billion in unclaimed property across more than 5 million accounts. One in ten Missourians has unclaimed property, with the average return being $300.

Search Missouri Unclaimed Property

Military Medals Program

The Missouri Treasurer's Office has recovered military medals and insignia from unclaimed safe deposit boxes. These valuable items are returned to veterans or their families at no cost.

Learn more about the Military Medals Program

Donate Your Unclaimed Property

Missouri offers a unique opportunity to donate unclaimed property to 20 different charitable organizations. The donation process can typically be completed entirely online.

Consider making a difference by donating your unclaimed property to a charitable cause.

How to Search for and Claim Your Property

Step 1: Search Online

Visit the Missouri State Treasurer's website to search the unclaimed property database.

Step 2: Sign Up for Alerts

Register for email notifications to be alerted when new property matching your information is reported to the state.

Step 3: File Your Claim

If you find property belonging to you, follow the online instructions to submit your claim and required documentation.
