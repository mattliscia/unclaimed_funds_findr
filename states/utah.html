Types of Unclaimed Property

Unclaimed property in Utah includes a variety of items such as:

  • Bank accounts
  • Utility deposits
  • Insurance benefits
  • Other financial assets

How It Works

When there is no activity on an account for a set period of time, the property is turned over to the state. The Utah State Treasurer's Office serves as the custodian until the property is claimed by the rightful owner.

Important: The process is free, secure, and straightforward!

How to Search and Claim Your Property

Step 1: Search the Database

Visit the Utah State Treasurer's website to search by name or business name.

Step 2: File Your Claim

Once you find unclaimed property, you can begin the claim process directly on the website.

Step 3: Provide Documentation

Submit the required documentation to verify your identity and ownership of the property.
