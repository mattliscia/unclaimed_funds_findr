Wyoming Unclaimed Property

The Wyoming State Treasurer's Office is responsible for managing the state's unclaimed property. Each year, millions of dollars in unclaimed funds are reported by businesses and institutions, and the state works diligently to reunite these funds with their rightful owners.

Search Wyoming Unclaimed Property

Types of Unclaimed Property

Unclaimed property can include a wide variety of assets such as:

  • Forgotten bank accounts
  • Uncashed checks
  • Insurance policy proceeds
  • Security deposits
  • Refunds
  • Stocks or bonds

How to Search and Claim Your Property

Step 1: Search the Database

Visit the Wyoming Unclaimed Property website to look for unclaimed property by entering your name or business name.

Step 2: File Your Claim

Once you locate unclaimed property that may belong to you, you can file a claim through the state's website. The claim process is straightforward and free of charge.

Step 3: Provide Documentation

Submit the required documentation to verify your identity and ownership of the property.
