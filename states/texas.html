Types of Unclaimed Property

Unclaimed property in Texas includes various items such as:

  • Bank accounts
  • Insurance benefits
  • Uncashed checks
  • Other financial assets

How It Works

These assets are considered unclaimed when there is no activity for a certain period, and they are transferred to the state. The Comptroller's office serves as the custodian of these funds until they are claimed by their rightful owners.

Important: The search and claim process is completely free!

How to Search and Claim Your Property

Step 1: Search the Database

Visit the Texas Comptroller's website to search for unclaimed property in Texas.

Step 2: Begin Your Claim

If you find property listed under your name, you can begin the claim process online. It's free, easy, and secure.

Step 3: Provide Documentation

Submit the required documentation to verify your identity and ownership of the property.
