What Is Unclaimed Property?

Unclaimed property typically includes:

  • Payroll checks
  • Checking & savings accounts
  • Safe deposit box contents
  • Life insurance proceeds
  • Stocks & dividends
  • Utility deposits & refunds

Financial institutions and businesses are required to turn over unclaimed assets after a period of inactivity.

How to Search for and Claim Your Property

Step 1: Search for Property

Use the Louisiana State Treasurer's Unclaimed Property Database to search by name.

Step 2: Submit a Claim

If you find property in your name, follow the instructions to claim it and provide proof of identity and ownership.

Step 3: Track Your Claim

Track the progress of your claim online through the Louisiana unclaimed property portal.
