Pennsylvania Unclaimed Property

The Pennsylvania Treasury's Bureau of Unclaimed Property works to reunite Pennsylvanians with their unclaimed property. With over $4 billion in unclaimed property currently being held, the Bureau serves as the custodian of this property until it can be returned to its rightful owners.

Types of Unclaimed Property

Common types of unclaimed property in Pennsylvania include:

  • Abandoned bank accounts
  • Uncashed checks
  • Lost stocks and bonds
  • Insurance policies
  • Contents of safe deposit boxes

How to Search and Claim Your Property

Step 1: Search the Database

Visit the Pennsylvania Treasury website to search by name or business name.

Step 2: File Your Claim

If you find property belonging to you, submit your claim through the secure online portal.

Step 3: Provide Documentation

Submit the required documentation to verify your identity and ownership of the property.

Important Information

The service is completely free - there is never a fee to claim your property

Property remains available indefinitely - there is no time limit to make your claim
