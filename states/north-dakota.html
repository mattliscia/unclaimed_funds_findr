North Dakota Unclaimed Property

The North Dakota Unclaimed Property Division, under the Department of Trust Lands, is committed to reuniting residents with their lost or misplaced assets. With over $118 million returned to rightful owners, the Division works to ensure unclaimed funds are returned in a timely manner.

Search North Dakota Unclaimed Property

Types of Unclaimed Property

Unclaimed property includes funds or items that have been left inactive for a period of time. Common types include:

  • Forgotten bank accounts
  • Uncashed checks
  • Insurance benefits
  • Security deposits

Business Education

The Division also focuses on educating businesses about the importance of reporting unclaimed property in accordance with state laws, ensuring compliance and proper handling of unclaimed assets.

How to Search and Claim Your Property

Step 1: Search the Database

Visit the North Dakota Unclaimed Property Division website to search by name or business name.

Step 2: Submit Your Claim

Once you locate your property, you can begin the claims process directly through the website.

Step 3: Provide Documentation

Submit the required documentation to verify your identity and ownership of the property.
