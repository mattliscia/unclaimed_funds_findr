Ohio Unclaimed Funds

The Ohio Department of Commerce's Division of Unclaimed Funds is dedicated to reuniting Ohioans with their forgotten or unclaimed assets. With a focus on transparency and diligent tracking, the Division helps ensure that hard-earned money and other forgotten assets are returned to their rightful owners.

Types of Unclaimed Funds

Unclaimed funds include money or property that has been abandoned or left inactive for a specified period of time. Common sources include:

  • Bank accounts
  • Rent or utility deposits
  • Uncashed checks
  • Undelivered stock certificates
  • Unclaimed insurance policies

How to Search and Claim Your Property

Step 1: Search the Database

Visit the Ohio Department of Commerce's Division of Unclaimed Funds website to search by name or business name.

Step 2: File Your Claim

If you find unclaimed funds belonging to you, follow the provided instructions to submit your claim.

Step 3: Provide Documentation

Submit the required documentation to verify your identity and ownership of the funds.
