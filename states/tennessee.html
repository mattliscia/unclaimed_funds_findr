Tennessee Unclaimed Property

The Tennessee Department of Treasury is committed to returning missing money to its rightful owners. Every year, millions of dollars in unclaimed property, including forgotten bank accounts, insurance benefits, and more, are transferred to the state. The Department works tirelessly to reunite people with their lost assets.

Search Tennessee Unclaimed Property

Types of Unclaimed Property

Unclaimed property includes assets such as:

  • Uncashed checks
  • Forgotten bank accounts
  • Insurance benefits
  • Other unclaimed financial assets

How It Works

These assets are held by the state after being inactive for a certain period of time. The Department of Treasury serves as the custodian of these funds until they are claimed by the rightful owners.

Important: The process is completely free of charge!

How to Search and Claim Your Property

Step 1: Search the Database

Visit ClaimItTN.gov to start your free search. Simply enter your name or business name.

Step 2: File Your Claim

Once you identify unclaimed property, you can easily file a claim online for free in just three simple steps.

Step 3: Verify Your Identity

Provide the required documentation to verify your identity and claim to the property.
