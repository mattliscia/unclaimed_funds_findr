Unclaimed Property in Delaware

The Delaware Office of Unclaimed Property oversees the state's efforts to reconnect individuals and businesses with lost or forgotten assets. Over the last three years, the office has successfully returned more than $1.5 billion to rightful owners.

Delaware is unique as the home of many businesses and corporations, making it a good idea to search for unclaimed property under both your personal and business names. Additionally, when searching in Delaware, unclaimed property from other states may also appear.

What Is Unclaimed Property?

Unclaimed property includes financial assets such as:

  • Forgotten bank accounts
  • Uncashed payroll checks
  • Refunds or rebates
  • Stocks, bonds, or dividends
  • Contents of safe deposit boxes

These assets are transferred to the state when the owner cannot be located after a period of inactivity.

How to Search for and Claim Your Property

Step 1: Search for Property

Visit Delaware's official unclaimed property website.

Enter your name, business name, or property ID number to start your search.

Step 2: Select Property and File a Claim

Review the search results and select the property(ies) you wish to claim. Begin the claims process by following the on-screen instructions.

Step 3: Review and Gather Documentation

Each claim requires a unique set of documents, which may include:

  • A completed, signed, and notarized claim form
  • A high-resolution, color copy of government-issued photo identification
  • Proof of your current mailing address
  • Documentation proving your connection to the property

You can find an initial list of required documents in Section C of the claim form you received. Additional documentation may be requested upon review.

Step 4: Submit Your Documentation

Delaware offers several options for submitting your documents:

  • Upload Securely: Use the state's secure document upload portal for the fastest processing
  • Mail Documents: Send your documentation to:

State of Delaware

Unclaimed Property Office

P.O. Box 8140

Wilmington, DE 19803-8140

Fax Documents: Fax your paperwork to 302-622-4115. Note: This option is only available for documents that do not require color copies.

Step 5: Track Your Claim

Monitor your claim status online at any time via Delaware's claim status portal.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Why search for business unclaimed property in Delaware?

Delaware is home to many corporations and LLCs. If you've incorporated a business in Delaware, it's worth checking for unclaimed property under your business name.

2. Can I find unclaimed property from other states in Delaware's database?

Yes, Delaware's search results may display unclaimed property from other states. If you spot an out-of-state claim, you will need to follow that state's specific claim process.

3. What are the fees for claiming property?

Claiming unclaimed property in Delaware is free. Be cautious of third-party services that charge fees for assistance.

4. Are there deadlines for claiming property?

No, unclaimed property remains with the state indefinitely until claimed.
