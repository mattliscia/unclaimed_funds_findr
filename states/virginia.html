Types of Unclaimed Property

Unclaimed property refers to any financial asset or personal property that has been abandoned by its owner. This could include:

  • Forgotten bank accounts
  • Uncashed checks
  • Insurance policy proceeds
  • Utility deposits
  • Stocks and bonds
  • Contents of safe deposit boxes

How to Search and Claim Your Property

Step 1: Search the Database

Visit the Virginia Unclaimed Property Search page. Simply enter your name or the name of a business to find out if there are any unclaimed assets.

Step 2: Submit Your Claim

If you find a match, you can submit a claim online through the Virginia Unclaimed Property portal.

Step 3: Provide Documentation

You will need to provide necessary documentation to verify your ownership (such as ID, proof of address, etc.).

Important Information

The claim process is free of charge - there is never a fee to claim your property

These assets are held by the state until the rightful owner is located
