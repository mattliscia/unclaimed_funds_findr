South Carolina Unclaimed Property

The South Carolina State Treasurer's Office, under the leadership of Curtis M. Loftis Jr., is dedicated to returning unclaimed property to its rightful owners. Millions of dollars are transferred to the Unclaimed Property Program every year by companies that are unable to locate the owners of various assets.

Search South Carolina Unclaimed Property

Who Can Have Unclaimed Property?

Various entities can have unclaimed property, including:

  • Individuals
  • Businesses
  • Estates
  • Churches and Charities

Types of Unclaimed Property

Common types of unclaimed property include:

  • Uncashed checks
  • Forgotten bank accounts
  • Tax refunds
  • Insurance benefits

How to Search and Claim Your Property

Step 1: Search the Database

Visit the South Carolina Unclaimed Property database and enter your name or business name.

Step 2: File Your Claim

If you find property belonging to you, follow the easy steps to file your claim. There is no cost to search or claim your property.

Step 3: Verify Your Identity

Provide the required documentation to prove your identity and claim to the property.
