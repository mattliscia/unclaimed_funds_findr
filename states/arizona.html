Unclaimed Property in Arizona

These funds come from abandoned accounts, uncashed checks, and more. Could some of it be yours? Arizona has partnered with MissingMoney.com, a government-approved resource, to make the claims process simple and secure.

What Is Unclaimed Property?

Unclaimed property includes financial assets that have been abandoned due to inactivity or lost contact with the owner. Common examples include:

  • Bank accounts or deposits
  • Uncashed checks, including payroll or refunds
  • Stock certificates or dividends
  • Insurance proceeds
  • Utility deposits

By law, these funds are transferred to the state after a certain period of inactivity. The Arizona Department of Revenue safeguards these assets until they are claimed by their rightful owner or heirs.

How to Search for and Claim Your Funds

Step 1: Conduct a Search

Visit Arizona's unclaimed property website to begin your search.

Enter your name, business name, or other identifying information to check for unclaimed assets.

Step 2: Complete the Claim on MissingMoney.com

After performing a search, Arizona's system redirects users to MissingMoney.com, an official government partner. You will complete the claims process on this trusted platform.

Step 3: File Your Claim

While the process is managed on MissingMoney.com, it typically involves:

  • Providing your personal information, including your current mailing address
  • Selecting your relationship to the property (e.g., owner or heir)
  • Submitting documentation to verify your identity and ownership

Step 4: Submit Required Documentation

Claims require specific documentation, which usually includes:

  • A completed and signed claim form
  • A copy of photo identification (e.g., driver's license or passport)
  • Proof of your social security number (e.g., SSN card, W-2 form)

Instructions for uploading or mailing documents are provided during the claims process.

Step 5: Check Your Claim Status

You can track the progress of your claim via MissingMoney.com. Simply log in to your account or use the claim tracking link provided in your confirmation email.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is there a fee to claim unclaimed property in Arizona?

No, the Arizona Department of Revenue and MissingMoney.com provide this service free of charge. Beware of third-party services that charge unnecessary fees.

2. How long does it take to process a claim?

Processing times vary based on the complexity of the claim and the documentation provided. Submitting complete and accurate documents speeds up the process.

3. What if I live outside of Arizona?

Even if you are no longer a resident, you can still claim funds held by the Arizona Department of Revenue if you are the rightful owner or heir.
