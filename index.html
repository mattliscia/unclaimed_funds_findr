Types of Unclaimed Funds & Resources

Unclaimed funds are financial assets that have been abandoned or forgotten, including old bank accounts, uncashed checks, insurance payouts, and more. Both you and the government benefit when these funds are returned—you receive your rightful money back, while states can reduce their administrative burden and fulfill their duty to reunite citizens with their assets.
